Salt And Pepper Short Rib

This is the recipe for asian style Salt And Pepper Short Rib.

Ingredients

Ingredient Name Quantity
Pork Rib 300 g
Garlic 5 cloves
Soy Sauce 20 ml
Grinded Black Pepper 5 g
Corn Starch 35 g
Water 20ml
Oil Amount needed for pan fry or deep fry the ribs

Directions

  1. Chop the pork ribs into 3 cm long pieces.
  2. Finely chop the garlic cloves
  3. Mix all the Ingredients by the following order: ribs, garlic, black pepper, soy sauce, corn starch, water.
  4. Marinate the ribs for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Pan fry or deep fry until the ribs are golden brown.
  6. Place the ribs on oil absorbing papper to filter out the oil.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
