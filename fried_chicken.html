Fried Chicken

This is the recipe for Fried Chicken.

Ingredients

Ingredient Name Quantity
Chicken Thigh 2 pieces
Garlic Powder 1/2 tsp
Canyee Pepper Powder 1/2 tsp
Grinded Black Pepper 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Flour Amount needed to fully bury a piece of chicken thigh
water 1 bowl
Oil Amount needed to deep frying a chicken thigh

Directions

  1. Mix the garlic powder, canyee pepper powder, black pepper, and salt.
  2. Spread the mix spices on the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Cover the chicken with a thin layer of flour, then put the chicken in the bowl of water and give it a quick bath.
  4. Put the chicken back to the flour and bury it with flour.
  5. Push the flour hard to make the flour stick to the chicken.
  6. Preheat the oil to 175 celsius degrees or 350 Fahrenheit degrees and deep fry the chicken for at least 15 minutes.
  7. Place the chicken on oil absorbing papper to filter out the oil.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
