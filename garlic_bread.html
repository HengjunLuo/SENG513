Garlic Bread

This is the recipe for Garlic Bread.

Ingredients

Ingredient Name Quantity
Toast 12 slices
Softened Butter 1/3 cups
Garlic 3 cloves
Salt 1/2 tsp
Fresh Parsley 15 g

Directions

  1. Finely chop the garlic cloves and parsley.
  2. Mix the chopped garlic and parsley with butter.
  3. Brush mixed butter on both sides of the toast slices.
  4. Pan fry the toast slices until both surfaces are golden brown and crisp.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
