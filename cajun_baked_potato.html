Salt And Pepper Short Rib

This is the recipe for asian style Salt And Pepper Short Rib

Ingredients

Ingredient Name Quantity
Large Potato 1
Garlic 3 cloves
Cajun Flavour Seasoning 10 g
Oil 20 ml

Directions

  1. Chop the pptato into bite-size pieces.
  2. Finely chop the garlic cloves.
  3. Mix the chopped garlic, cajun seasoning, and oil.
  4. Boil the chopped potato for 10 minutes.
  5. Filter out the water and mix the potato with the seasonings.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 Fahrenheit degrees.
  7. Bake the potato for 15 minutes, take it out and flip over each pieces.
  8. Bake for another 5 minutes.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
